Frequently asked questions

For food lovers 🍕

What exactly is Tasty Passport?

Tasty Passport is a web app that gives you instant discounts and level-based rewards at selected cafes and restaurants across Europe.

Do I need to install an app?

No installs. You just tap the link, sign in, and start using your discounts from your browser.

How do discounts and levels work?

Each venue has a welcome offer and a simple ladder. The more times you visit and redeem, the better the rewards get for that specific place.

Can I use it in more than one city?

Yes. Your Tasty Passport travels with you — you can switch cities anytime and keep your levels per venue.

For venues 🏢

How do we validate discounts at the counter?

Guests show a QR code at checkout. You scan it and the system logs the visit and the reward in real time.

Do we control our own offers?

Yes. You design your welcome offer and the reward ladder — coffee, dessert, secret item, whatever fits your margin.

What do we see in the dashboard?

You see new vs returning guests, redemptions per reward level, and basic insights to understand what actually drives loyalty.

How does pilot access work?

Pilot venues get concierge onboarding, featured placement in the city view, and priority access to new analytics features.
